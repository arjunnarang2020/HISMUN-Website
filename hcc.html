Executive Board Members

Chairperson

Arjun Narang

With experience in over ten Model United Nations conferences, Arjun Narang brings a wealth of diplomatic and leadership expertise to the Historic Crisis Committee at Harvest MUN 2025. Known for his analytical approach and confident diplomacy, he strives to create an immersive and intellectually stimulating crisis experience. As Chair, he aims to foster meaningful debate, critical thinking, and creativity among delegates. Arjun believes that every committee session should not only challenge participants but also inspire them to see diplomacy through a new lens.

Co-Chairperson

Sahil Agrawal

Sahil Agrawal is a highly experienced MUN enthusiast known for his sharp intellect, confident diplomacy, and strong leadership skills. With years of experience in various committees, he has developed a deep understanding of global affairs and excels at articulating complex ideas with clarity and charisma. Beyond the committee room, Sahil is just a really cool individual, admired for his calm demeanor and sense of humor. As a passionate basketball player, he brings the same teamwork, strategy, and determination from the court into every challenge he faces. Sahil truly embodies intellect, versatility, and effortless charm like no other.
